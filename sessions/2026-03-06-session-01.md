## 🧠 How the Voice Actually Works (Critical Understanding)

```
User speaks Hindi → Whisper converts to text
                  → LLaMA generates Hindi response text
                  → VITS (trained on your voice) speaks that text
                  → Output: YOUR voice saying the response
```

The model doesn't store sentences. It learns your **vocal patterns** — tone, rhythm, accent — and can speak ANY new text in your style.

**Training is 2-phase:**
1. Train VITS on **IndicTTS Hindi corpus** → good Hindi TTS base
2. Fine-tune on **YOUR 30 min recordings** → YOUR voice

---

## 🔄 The Data Flywheel (Key Insight from Session)

Every recording session creates data for **BOTH** models simultaneously:

```
You read Hindi text aloud → audio + transcript saved
  ↓
  ├── TTS training data   (learn your voice)
  └── ASR training data   (learn to understand your speech)
```

**One session. Two models improving. Forever.**

### ASR Strategy (3 Layers)
1. **MVP:** Whisper large-v3 as-is (~85-90% Hindi accuracy)
2. **Phase 2:** Fine-tune Whisper on YOUR recordings → 93-95%
3. **Year 2-5:** Correction loop + diverse data → world-class Hindi ASR

### Correction Loop (Product Feature)
- When Whisper mishears you → you correct it in the UI
- Each correction = 1 training sample automatically saved
- 1000 corrections = dramatically better Hindi ASR

## 📱 MVP Scope

### 4 Tabs in the Web App
1. **📼 Record** — Shows Hindi prompts → you read → saves audio+transcript
2. **🔬 Analyze** — Audio quality, phoneme coverage, what's missing
3. **⚙️ Train** — One button → triggers VITS fine-tuning → live progress
4. **🎙️ Talk** — Push to talk → full pipeline → YOUR voice responds

### MVP Features
- Hindi voice recording with transcript
- Audio quality analysis
- VITS training on your voice
- Talk to assistant in Hindi
- LLaMA answers via Ollama
- YouTube audio playback ("gaana bajao")

### Not in MVP
- Raspberry Pi deployment
- OpenAI integration
- Legacy/time-locked messages for daughter
- Multi-user / product distribution
